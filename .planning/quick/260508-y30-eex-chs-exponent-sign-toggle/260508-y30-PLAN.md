<objective>
Fix EEX+CHS exponent sign entry so negative exponents can be typed.

Purpose: Previously pressing 'n' (CHS) during EEX entry flushed entry_buf and negated X on
the stack, producing wrong results (e.g. -1.0 instead of 1E-2). The HP-41 hardware toggles the
exponent sign in-place without committing the number.

Output: Two-part fix — intercept in app.rs (Part A) and normalization in ops/mod.rs (Part B),
plus unit and integration tests covering toggle, round-trip, and flush edge cases.
</objective>
